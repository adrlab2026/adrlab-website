Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data.

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all ADR Lab services including the main website (adresearchlab.com). It is designed to comply with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who We Are

ADR Lab ("we", "us", "our") is an academic support company providing final year project development, research paper guidance, and career services for engineering students worldwide. For privacy matters, contact us at adrlab2026@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Enquiry & Booking Data

When you submit the booking form or contact us, we collect your name, contact number or email, college name, and project requirements.

2.2 Communication Data

Messages exchanged via WhatsApp, email, or our contact form are retained for the duration of our service engagement and up to 12 months after.

2.3 Technical Data

Standard website analytics data (page visits, browser type, approximate location) via Google Analytics. This data is anonymised and not linked to your personal identity.

3. How We Use Your Data

4. What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data to any third party
  • We never share your project requirements with other students
  • We never use your project work for any other purpose
  • We never send unsolicited marketing messages

5. Data Retention

  • Project-related communication — retained for 12 months after delivery
  • Analytics data — retained for 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, email adrlab2026@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store card or payment details — all payments are processed by Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via our website or email at least 14 days before they take effect.
