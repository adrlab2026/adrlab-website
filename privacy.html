Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all ADR Lab products including ADR Scholar (scholar.adresearchlab.com) and the main website (adresearchlab.com). It complies with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Amendment) Act 2008, and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who We Are

ADR Lab ("we", "us", "our") is an academic technology company that builds AI-powered tools for engineering students, institutions, and companies worldwide. Our registered contact for privacy matters is [email protected].

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

When you create an account on ADR Scholar, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a one-way BCrypt hash — we cannot read your password)
  • Subscription plan (FREE or PRO)
  • Account creation date

2.2 Usage Data

To enforce plan limits and prevent abuse, we collect:

  • Number of idea generations per month
  • Number of template downloads per month
  • Number of research tool uses per month
  • Date and time of last activity

2.3 Payment Data

We use Razorpay as our payment processor. We do not store your card details, UPI ID, or banking information. Razorpay processes all payments and provides us only with a transaction confirmation, order ID, and payment status. Razorpay is PCI-DSS compliant.

2.4 Content You Generate

When you use ADR Scholar to generate ideas, templates, or research content, your inputs (domain, tech stack, project details) are sent to the Claude AI API (Anthropic) to generate responses. We do not permanently store the content of your generations beyond what is displayed to you in the session.

2.5 Technical Data

We may collect standard technical data including IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited for security purposes and to improve the service. This data is not linked to your personal profile and is retained for a maximum of 30 days.

4. What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • We never use your generated content to train AI models
  • We never share your data with advertisers
  • We never send marketing emails without your explicit consent
  • We never store payment card details on our servers

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate ADR Scholar:

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI content generation. Your prompts are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Razorpay — Payment processing. Razorpay's privacy policy applies: razorpay.com/privacy
  • Railway — Backend hosting (India/US region servers)
  • Vercel — Frontend hosting (global CDN)

6. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained as long as your account is active
  • Usage tracking data — retained for 12 months then deleted
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law
  • Technical/log data — retained for 30 days then automatically deleted

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR (for users in the European Economic Area and UK)

  • Right of access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to restrict processing — Limit how we use your data

Under Indian IT Act (for users in India)

  • Right to access and review your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to withdraw consent for non-essential data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher)
  • Passwords hashed using BCrypt with salt (industry standard)
  • JWT tokens with 7-day expiry for session management
  • Database access restricted to application servers only
  • API keys stored as environment variables, never in code

9. Cookies

ADR Scholar uses only essential functional cookies required for authentication (JWT token stored in browser localStorage). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

ADR Scholar is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has created an account, please contact us at [email protected] and we will delete the account promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside India (specifically in the United States via Railway and Vercel infrastructure). For GDPR compliance, these transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions where applicable.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or a prominent notice on our website at least 30 days before the change takes effect. The date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last updated.
