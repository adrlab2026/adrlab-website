About ADR Lab

We help engineering students finish their final year projects and research papers — with expertise, integrity, and genuine care.

Our Story

ADR Lab was founded with one clear observation — engineering students in India and around the world spend an enormous amount of time on documentation, research, and project paperwork, leaving very little time for actual learning and building.

Final year project submissions, research papers, SRS documents, and synopsis reports all demand hours of effort that most students are not trained to handle efficiently. We saw students struggling not because they lacked intelligence or ability, but because they lacked the right tools and guidance.

ADR Lab was built to fix this. We combine deep academic expertise with modern tools to give every student a fair shot at submitting professional-quality work — regardless of their background or the quality of mentorship they have access to.

From a single-service operation helping local students, we've grown into a multi-domain academic support platform serving students across India and worldwide — with 500+ projects delivered and a 4.9★ average rating.

What Guides Us

Our Values

Every decision we make is filtered through these four principles.

🎯

Student First

Every decision starts with one question — does this make a student's life easier? We build features because students need them, not to impress investors.

💰

Accessible Pricing

Most of our users are students with limited budgets. Our pricing is intentionally affordable — far below international competitors — while remaining sustainable.

📚

Academic Integrity

We help students work smarter, not cheat. All our support is a starting point that students review, edit, and personalise — making it genuinely their own work.

🔒

Privacy by Design

We collect only the minimum data needed. We never sell student data or use your content to train AI models. Privacy is built into everything we ship.
