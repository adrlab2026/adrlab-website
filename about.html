About ADR Lab

We build AI-powered academic tools that help engineering students worldwide finish their projects faster — without stress.

Our Story

ADR Lab was founded with one clear observation — engineering students in India and around the world spend an enormous amount of time on documentation, research, and project paperwork, leaving very little time for actual learning and building.

Final year project submissions, research papers, SRS documents, and synopsis reports all demand hours of effort that most students are not trained to handle efficiently. We saw students struggling not because they lacked intelligence or ability, but because they lacked the right tools and guidance.

ADR Lab was built to fix this. We combine the power of modern AI with deep understanding of academic requirements to give every student a fair shot at submitting professional-quality work — regardless of their background or the quality of mentorship they have access to.

Our Mission

"Finish Your Final Year Project Faster — Without Stress."

We believe every engineering student deserves access to high-quality academic tools. Our mission is to democratize academic success by making AI-powered project assistance accessible, affordable, and genuinely useful — for students from tier-1 colleges and tier-3 colleges alike, in India and across the world.

What We Build

ADR Lab is building a complete ecosystem of academic tools under one umbrella brand:

  • ADR Scholar — AI tools for BTech and MTech students. Project idea generator, SRS and synopsis templates, AI-written project reports, and a full research paper writing assistant supporting IEEE, Springer, and APA formats.
  • ADR Check — Plagiarism detection and AI-content detection tool. A Turnitin alternative built specifically for the Indian and global academic market, at a fraction of the cost.
  • ADR Campus — Institution-facing portal for colleges to manage student projects, guide assignments, and bulk plagiarism checking.
  • ADR Ventures — R&D collaboration tools for companies working with academic institutions.
  • ADR Connect — Alumni network connecting graduates with current students for mentorship.
  • ADR Bridge — Placement bridge connecting students with companies through verified project portfolios.

Our Values

Student First

Every decision we make starts with one question — does this make a student's life easier? We do not build features to impress investors or competitors. We build features because students need them.

Accessible Pricing

We are deeply aware that most of our users are students with limited budgets. Our pricing is intentionally designed to be affordable — far below international competitors — while ensuring we remain sustainable as a business.

Academic Integrity

We build tools that help students work smarter, not tools that help them cheat. Our AI-generated content is always intended as a starting draft — we encourage every student to review, edit, and personalise what our tools produce before submission.

Privacy by Design

We collect only the data we absolutely need. We never sell student data. We never use student content to train AI models without explicit consent. Privacy is not an afterthought — it is built into every product we ship.
