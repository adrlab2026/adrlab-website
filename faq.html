Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ADR Lab's project, research, and career services.

🏠 General
What is ADR Lab?

ADR Lab is an academic project and research support company founded in 2024. We help engineering students — B.Tech, M.Tech, BCA, MCA, and Research Scholars — complete their final year projects, research papers, and career documentation.

We provide end-to-end services: from project idea selection and full development to research paper writing, resume creation, LinkedIn optimisation, and mock interview coaching.

Who can use ADR Lab's services?

Our services are designed for:

  • UG students (B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc) working on final year projects
  • PG students (M.Tech, MCA, M.Sc) needing research or project guidance
  • Research scholars requiring paper writing and journal submission support
  • Professionals seeking resume, LinkedIn, or interview preparation help

We serve students and professionals across India and worldwide.

What domains do you support for final year projects?

We currently support projects across the following domains:

  • AI / ML & Deep Learning — TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, OpenCV
  • Java / Spring Boot — REST APIs, Hibernate, Microservices
  • Blockchain — Smart contracts, Solidity, DApps
  • Cybersecurity — Ethical hacking, IDS, network security
  • Full Stack — React, Node.js, Django, Flask
  • Data Science — Data analysis, visualisation, prediction models
  • Java + AI/ML Hybrid — Spring Boot + Python ML microservices
Are you available for students outside India?

Yes. ADR Lab serves students worldwide. We have supported students from India, the UAE, Malaysia, the UK, and other countries. Our communication is entirely online via WhatsApp, email, and video calls, so location is never a barrier.

💻 Final Year Projects
What does "complete project development" include?

Our complete project development package typically includes:

  • Project topic selection and abstract
  • Full source code (frontend + backend + ML model if applicable)
  • Database design and setup
  • Project documentation — SRS, synopsis, and project report
  • Deployment support (if required)
  • Viva/presentation preparation guidance
  • Post-delivery support for 2 weeks
How long does project development take?

Timelines depend on the project complexity:

  • Simple projects (basic CRUD, Java Spring Boot) — 5–7 days
  • Medium projects (Full stack, ML model) — 10–15 days
  • Complex projects (Blockchain, federated learning, advanced AI) — 15–25 days

We always confirm the timeline before starting. If you have an urgent deadline, mention it upfront — we do our best to accommodate.

Will my project be unique / not submitted before?

Yes. Every project is built fresh based on your specific requirements and base paper. We do not resell the same project to multiple students. Your implementation, dataset, and codebase are unique to your submission.

Do you provide the base paper / research paper for the project?

We can help you find a suitable IEEE or Springer base paper for your project domain. Alternatively, if your college requires a specific paper, you can share it with us and we will implement the system described in it.

🔬 Research Paper Guidance
What research paper services do you offer?
  • Full research paper writing (IEEE, Springer, APA format)
  • Abstract and literature review writing
  • Paper editing, proofreading, and formatting
  • Plagiarism reduction and paraphrasing
  • Journal and conference identification and submission guidance
  • Methodology and results section writing
Can you guarantee journal acceptance?

No ethical service can guarantee journal acceptance, as it depends on peer review which is entirely outside our control. What we can guarantee is a professionally written, formatted, and proofread paper that meets the technical and structural requirements of your target journal or conference.

💼 Career Services
What is included in the resume service?

Our resume service includes:

  • Complete rewrite in ATS-friendly format
  • Role-specific keyword optimisation
  • Clean, professional design (Word + PDF delivered)
  • Unlimited revisions until you are satisfied
  • Delivery within 24–48 hours
How does LinkedIn optimization work?

You share your current LinkedIn profile URL and target job role. We then rewrite your headline, about section, experience descriptions, and skills section with keywords that increase your search ranking for recruiters. We also advise on profile photo, banner, and featured section strategy.

How is the mock interview conducted?

Mock interviews are conducted via Google Meet or Zoom. A domain expert conducts a 45–60 minute session simulating a real HR + technical interview. After the session, you receive a written feedback report covering your strengths, areas to improve, and suggested answers for questions you struggled with.

💰 Pricing & Process
How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on the service type and complexity. We intentionally keep prices student-friendly. To get an exact quote for your specific requirement, reach out via WhatsApp or use the booking form on our homepage — we respond with a proposal within 24 hours.

As a general guide: simple Java projects start from ₹2,000, AI/ML projects from ₹3,500, and full-stack hybrid projects from ₹5,000. Research papers and career services are priced separately.

What is the payment process?

We typically work on a 50% advance, 50% on delivery model. Payment can be made via UPI, bank transfer, or any digital payment method. We share a payment confirmation before starting work.

Is my project information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We treat every project with complete confidentiality. Your project details, source code, research content, and personal information are never shared with any third party. We do not resell or reuse your work in any form.

What if I'm not satisfied with the delivered work?

We offer revision support as part of every delivery. If the work does not meet the agreed requirements, we will revise it until it does. We ask that feedback be specific and based on the original requirements shared at the time of booking. Our goal is for every student to be genuinely satisfied with their project.
